Welcome to Surveyli

Your tiny tool for sharing surveys with your friends.

By creating a survey you will get a list of the next two weeks where you select the dates that suit you best. We all know friends who say they will join on a certain date, but then when the day comes closer they suddenly cannot show up because the date was just too far away or they probably forgot it. With this approach events can be started earlier and they are more likely to happen for real.

Cave

For evaluating a survey personal data gets processed, especially your first name, last name and the selected dates where you are available for the event. With using this tool you agree that this data may be stored permanently. Finished surveys will be deleted automatically at the day after the end of the survey, this does also include your personal data.
